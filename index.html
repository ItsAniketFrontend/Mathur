Introduction

Ar. Ashish Mathur, is a very well known architect, who has done projects Pan India. Ar. Ashish Mathur holds a Bachelors in Architecture degree from Vastu Kala Academy, College of Architecture, New Delhi. With over 20+ years of rich experience he has done over a 1000 projects, which comprise of airports, railway stations, commercial buildings, master planning, warehousing, industrial buildings, auditoriums, landscapping and corperate interiors for reputed organizations.

Library:

Welcome to the "Circle of Wisdom Library," an architectural marvel conceived for the pursuit of knowledge. Envisioned for an esteemed competition, our design revolves around the concept that knowledge completes life, much like a circle completes its 360 degrees. The library unfolds in a circular layout, symbolizing the endless journey of acquiring wisdom. Each section is thoughtfully curved, creating an organic flow that fosters exploration and discovery.

Interior: (residence)

Welcome to a modern masterpiece! This residence features four rooms, a duplex living and dining area, and a central courtyard, with a staircase that elevates the design. The elegant elevation combines Italian beige stones, glass facades, and wooden textures, enhanced by profile lights for a touch of linear sophistication. Stilt parking adds practicality, while upper-floor living areas provide an elevated living experience.

Theatre:

Step into the epitome of cinematic indulgence at "LuxeScreen Cinemas." Our theater interior is a symphony of sophistication and luxury, enveloped in a palette of refined grey shades. Immerse yourself in the opulent ambiance enhanced by linear dim lights, meticulously designed for optimal theater lighting. As you recline in plush seating, the soothing glow and carefully curated surroundings create an atmosphere of unparalleled comfort.

Our Working Process

01

Developing a concept

Our process commences with the creation of original, top-tier concept designs tailored to our clients' briefs. We adhere to a professional ethos, prioritizing the delivery of solutions that align seamlessly with our clients' needs and preferences

02

Design Process

The subsequent phase in our workflow centers around the intricacies of our design methodology. Initiating this process involves solidifying layouts, seamlessly progressing to elevation design or 3D rendering of spaces, tailored to the specific demands of the project.

03

Budget Planning

Prior to embarking on the execution phase, we ensure alignment with our clients by conclusively determining the budget. This collaborative process involves finalizing materials that seamlessly fit within the established budget parameters.

04

Constructing the vision

Embracing a professional methodology, we navigate the execution of projects with precision, mitigating common pitfalls. This approach ensures the avoidance of errors while strategically selecting the optimal methods to bring your ideas to fruition.
