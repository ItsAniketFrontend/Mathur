Residential

Pent House:

The elevation, a symphony of symmetrical design with randomized linear extrusions, effortlessly navigates the site's complexities. These continuous extrusions, meticulously lit along the edges, create a dynamic interplay of light, material, and varying shades of grey, imbuing "Forge Haven" with a refined and sophisticated character. A testament to resilience and thoughtful design, this penthousel stands as a beacon of strength and elegance in its unique urban landscape.

TV unit:

Metallic stripes gracefully accentuate the contours, adding a bold, loud statement to the design. The unit is thoughtfully illuminated with cove lights, creating a cinematic ambiance that enhances your viewing experience. Complementing this, a rounded or curved-edged vanity or table completes the ensemble, harmonizing with the fluid lines and lavish aesthetic of the "CinéLuxe Console." Elevate your home entertainment with a touch of glamour and sophistication.

Bedroom interior (new):

The flooring, a blend of wooden and Italian stone, radiates sophistication. Minimalist-inspired false ceilings and a hanging light fixture add an artistic touch, while glass wardrobes with translucent panels create an airy ambiance. The color palette, a seamless mix of soothing beige and rich wood textures, sets the tone for a tranquil yet contemporary retreat. Welcome to a space where modernity meets refined design.

Library:

Welcome to the "Circle of Wisdom Library," an architectural marvel conceived for the pursuit of knowledge. Our Working Process

01

Developing a concept

Our process commences with the creation of original, top-tier concept designs tailored to our clients' briefs. We adhere to a professional ethos, prioritizing the delivery of solutions that align seamlessly with our clients' needs and preferences

02

Design Process

The subsequent phase in our workflow centers around the intricacies of our design methodology. Initiating this process involves solidifying layouts, seamlessly progressing to elevation design or 3D rendering of spaces, tailored to the specific demands of the project.

03

Budget Planning

Prior to embarking on the execution phase, we ensure alignment with our clients by conclusively determining the budget. This collaborative process involves finalizing materials that seamlessly fit within the established budget parameters.

04

Constructing the vision

Embracing a professional methodology, we navigate the execution of projects with precision, mitigating common pitfalls. This approach ensures the avoidance of errors while strategically selecting the optimal methods to bring your ideas to fruition.
