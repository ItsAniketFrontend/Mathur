Commercial Tower

Our glass-clad masterpiece boasts a façade adorned with triangular lighted panels in sleek metal, harmoniously illuminated by stripes of light, delivering a captivating visual symphony. Elevate your business presence on our upper floors, meticulously designed as exclusive offices that radiate refinement. Ascend to our rooftop oasis, home to a prestigious restaurant offering unparalleled city views.

Residential

Discover the unique charm of our one story building elevation, a harmonious blend of creativity and functionality. MS bars run seamlessly from floor to floor, outlining a circular cut that frames a flourishing tree—a living, breathing centerpiece. At the heart of the facade stands a box frame, adorned with a captivating mix of bricks, stones, and a glass rail, creating a visually arresting focal point.

Interior

Carefully designed to maximize space and utility, each corner of the house embodies functionality without compromising style. The façade, treated with a modern and dynamic touch, captures the essence of innovation. Despite its small budget, this residence stands as a testament to smart design, offering an affordable yet stylish living space. Experience the perfect fusion of form and function, where simplicity meets sophistication in a dwelling that transcends budget constraints.

Pent House:

The elevation, a symphony of symmetrical design with randomized linear extrusions, effortlessly navigates the site's complexities. These continuous extrusions, meticulously lit along the edges, create a dynamic interplay of light, material, and varying shades of grey, imbuing "Forge Haven" with a refined and sophisticated character. A testament to resilience and thoughtful design, this penthousel stands as a beacon of strength and elegance in its unique urban landscape.

TV unit:

Metallic stripes gracefully accentuate the contours, adding a bold, loud statement to the design. The unit is thoughtfully illuminated with cove lights, creating a cinematic ambiance that enhances your viewing experience. Complementing this, a rounded or curved-edged vanity or table completes the ensemble, harmonizing with the fluid lines and lavish aesthetic of the "CinéLuxe Console." Elevate your home entertainment with a touch of glamour and sophistication.

Bedroom interior (new):

The flooring, a blend of wooden and Italian stone, radiates sophistication. Minimalist-inspired false ceilings and a hanging light fixture add an artistic touch, while glass wardrobes with translucent panels create an airy ambiance. The color palette, a seamless mix of soothing beige and rich wood textures, sets the tone for a tranquil yet contemporary retreat. Welcome to a space where modernity meets refined design.

Library:

Welcome to the "Circle of Wisdom Library," an architectural marvel conceived for the pursuit of knowledge. Envisioned for an esteemed competition, our design revolves around the concept that knowledge completes life, much like a circle completes its 360 degrees. The library unfolds in a circular layout, symbolizing the endless journey of acquiring wisdom. Each section is thoughtfully curved, creating an organic flow that fosters exploration and discovery.

Interior: (residence)

Stilt parking adds practicality, while upper-floor living areas provide an elevated living experience.
