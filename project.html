Library:

Welcome to the "Circle of Wisdom Library," an architectural marvel conceived for the pursuit of knowledge. Envisioned for an esteemed competition, our design revolves around the concept that knowledge completes life, much like a circle completes its 360 degrees. The library unfolds in a circular layout, symbolizing the endless journey of acquiring wisdom. Each section is thoughtfully curved, creating an organic flow that fosters exploration and discovery.

Interior: (residence)

Welcome to a modern masterpiece! This residence features four rooms, a duplex living and dining area, and a central courtyard, with a staircase that elevates the design. The elegant elevation combines Italian beige stones, glass facades, and wooden textures, enhanced by profile lights for a touch of linear sophistication. Stilt parking adds practicality, while upper-floor living areas provide an elevated living experience.

Theatre:

Step into the epitome of cinematic indulgence at "LuxeScreen Cinemas." Our theater interior is a symphony of sophistication and luxury, enveloped in a palette of refined grey shades. Immerse yourself in the opulent ambiance enhanced by linear dim lights, meticulously designed for optimal theater lighting. As you recline in plush seating, the soothing glow and carefully curated surroundings create an atmosphere of unparalleled comfort.
